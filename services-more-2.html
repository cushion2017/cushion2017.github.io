Cognitive Therapy

Helps with thinking problems and recurring episodes of depression, Teaches people how to react to thoughts and feelings. Being similar to MDT, It is based on an educational model where people in therapy are helped to move on from negative thoughts and reaction. By challenge situations by breaking down problems into manageable parts an individual can then focus on setting and reaching short term goal. Attitudes are then able to be adjusted with a positive thought of mind through adjusting their attitude to specific issues. Practicing the most appropriate way in approaching a problem can be lost in the raising a child creating further difficulties as they grow into teenagers. Below are guidelines in teaching these methods to overcome problems in daily lif. A note pad will be need for this class exercises


Techniques in location problems


1. Writing a self statement for counteracting negative thoughts


2. Finding opportunities to think positively, If you hate something about a room for example, choose 5 things to say whats positive about it.


3. Visualise the best part of everyday and wright them down


4. Learn to accept disappointments as a normal part of daily life, To feel it and realise that the problem is now out of your control.


Ask the following questions:
What are you in control of?
What Happened?
What have you learned from the problem?
What can you do differently next time?
