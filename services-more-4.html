HEADSET GEAR INSTRUCTIONS

Gear VR Hardware Setup


First, install and adjust the Gear VR straps.

1 | Insert the ends of the main strap (the wider strap) through the loops on the Gear VR headset. Fasten the Velcro tabs to ensure a snug but comfortable fit.

2 | Fit the hook at the end of top strap under the metal bar at the top of the headset, then gently pull upward until it snaps into place.

3 | Slide the other end of top strap through the loop in the main strap and fasten the Velcro tab.

4 | Adjust the straps until the headset rests comfortably on your head, with the lenses centered in front of your eyes.

5 | Gently slide the device holder into the correct position.

6 | Hold the mobile device at a slight angle to slide the headset.

7 | Press the top of the mobile device into the device holder until it clicks.

8 | Make sure the screen of the mobile device faces inward, toward the Gear VR headset.
